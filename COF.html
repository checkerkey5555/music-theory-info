ABOUT THE CIRCLE OF FIFTHS 
						 
The circle of fifths shows the pitches in a order of perfect fifths in 
the 12 tone equal temperament, generally shown as a circle with the pitches 
(and their corresponding keys) in a clockwise progression. Musicians and 
composers often use the circle of fifths to describe the musical
relationships between pitches. Its design is helpful in composing and 
harmonising melodies, building chords, and modulating to different keys 
within a composition.						 

it can be used for key changes as the keys next to each other are very 
close only changing one pitch at a time and it can help you with your
2-5-1s as 2-5-1s are technically just going through the circle of 
fifths from a anti clockwise direction
